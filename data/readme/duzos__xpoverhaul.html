Enchanting Overhaul

A ground-up rework of Minecraft's enchanting and experience.

a commission, by Duzo

What is it?

A Fabric mod that overhauls how enchanting and experience work, reworking the systems around the anvil, experience orbs, and item handling rather than just adding new content.

Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.20.4 (Fabric)
