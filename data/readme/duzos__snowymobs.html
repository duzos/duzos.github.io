Snowy Mobs

Mobs that gather snow as they wander the cold.

a commission, by Duzo & Loqor

What is it?

A mob that collects snow over time, building up a snowy layer that shows on the mob itself. The longer it spends out in the cold, the snowier it gets.

Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.20.1
  • Forge
