Beyond the End

Ender's Journey — what waits past the End.

a commission, by Duzo

What is it?

A Forge mod that opens up new places beyond the End — extra dimensions to travel to, each with its own custom skybox and atmosphere.

Features

  • New dimensions to explore past the End.
  • Custom skyboxes for the new realms.
  • Atmospheric sound design.

⚠️ Alpha (0.3.1-alpha) — a work in progress.

Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.19.2
  • Forge
