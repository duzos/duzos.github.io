Merseyrail · Railway 200

A static website celebrating 200 years of the railway, built around Merseyrail.

What is it?

An educational website made for Railway 200 - the bicentenary of the modern railway (1825-2025) - and KGV's Boss-It week. It introduces Railway 200, showcases Merseyrail, and walks through the history of the trains that have run on the network.

Live: duzo.is-a.dev/merseyrail-site

What's on the site

  • Railway 200 - what the anniversary is and why it matters.
  • The Inspiration train - the touring exhibition and its three themes: railway firsts, Wonderlab on Wheels, and railway careers.
  • Merseyrail careers - information and a link through to the Merseyrail careers hub.
  • Ticket carousel - a browse-through of tickets.
  • Train history - a gallery of Merseyrail-era trains (the 54, 502, 507, 777, and 1904) with diagrams, photos, and departure sounds.

Built with

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no build step or framework. The site uses a custom "Ticketing" typeface and self-hosted media.

Running locally

Clone the repo and open index.html in a browser:

git clone https://github.com/duzos/merseyrail-site.git

For the audio and video to load reliably, serve it from a simple static server instead, e.g.:

npx serve .
# or
python -m http.server
