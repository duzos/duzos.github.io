Time Lord regeneration for the Origins mod.

by Duzo

Requires Cwaigs Regeneration.

What is it?

An add-on that ties Doctor Who-style regeneration into the Origins mod. Die, regenerate, and maybe come back as someone else entirely.

With Origins

  • Adds the Time Lord origin, whose single power is regeneration.
  • Adds the Regeneration power: 12 regenerations, and on your final one you're given a random origin.
  • If you regenerate while you aren't a Time Lord, your body is unstable and your origin changes at random.

With Pehkui

  • Randomly changes your size when you regenerate. (More planned.)

Config

Every feature is toggleable and tweakable in-game via a config - this requires Mod Menu.
