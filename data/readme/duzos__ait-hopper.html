Tardis Hopper

One item. Straight to your TARDIS interior.

an Adventures in Time addon, by Duzo

What is it?

A small add-on for Adventures in Time (AIT) that adds an item to teleport you straight to your linked TARDIS' interior — no walking back to the doors.

Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.20.1 (Fabric)
  • Adventures in Time
