What does it add?

🧀 Cheese

  • An edible, sustainable food source.
  • Crafted from sponges in a crafting table.
  • Also doubles as explosive ammunition.
  • Cheese is good.

🚀 Cheese Bazooka

  • Shoots cheese — but only if you've got cheese in your inventory.
  • The cheese it fires is explosive. Boom.
  • Comes with a very cheesy custom model.
  • Crafted by surrounding a crossbow and TNT with cheese.
